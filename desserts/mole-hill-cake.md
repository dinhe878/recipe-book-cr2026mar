# Mole Hill Cake

## Ingredients: 
- 1 box chocolate cake mix
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup flour

## Instructions: 
1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. In a large bowl, combine the chocolate cake mix, milk, sugar, butter, and flour. Mix until well blended.
3. Pour the batter into a greased 9x13 inch baking pan.
4. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
5. Allow the cake to cool before serving. Enjoy your delicious Mole Hill Cake!
