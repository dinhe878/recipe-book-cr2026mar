# Pasta boscaiola recipe


## Ingredients

- rigatoni
- pancetta
- peas
- mushrooms
- onions


## Instructions

- Boil pasta in a large pot of of well-salted water (to taste, but 3-5 g/L is a good starting point).
- Roast mushrooms, sauté onions, add pancetta and onions.
- Toss all together, and enjoy
