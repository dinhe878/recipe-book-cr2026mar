# Spaghetti aglio e olio

## Ingredients
1. 200 g spaghetti
2. 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
3. 1/4 cup (60 ml) extra virgin olive oil
4. 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
5. Salt, to taste
6. Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)
7. Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

## Instructions
1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water, then drain the pasta.
2. While the pasta cooks, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Sauté for 1–2 minutes until the garlic is golden and fragrant (be careful not to burn it).
3. Add the drained spaghetti to the skillet and toss to coat in the garlic oil. If the pasta seems dry, add a little of the reserved pasta water to loosen it up.
4. Season with salt to taste and toss again.
5. Remove from heat and stir in chopped parsley if using.
6. Serve immediately, topped with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
