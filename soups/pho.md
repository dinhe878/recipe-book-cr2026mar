# Pho

## Ingredients

- 500 g beef bones (marrow or knuckle)
- 300 g beef brisket or flank
- 1 onion, halved
- 1 ginger, sliced
- 2 star anise
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 3 cloves
- 1 cardamom pod
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp salt
- 200 g rice noodles
- Toppings: sliced beef, fresh herbs (cilantro, Thai basil), bean sprouts, lime wedges, chili, hoisin sauce

## Instructions

- Char the onion and ginger over an open flame or in a hot pan until slightly blackened.
- In a large pot, cover beef bones and brisket with water. Bring to a boil, then discard water and rinse bones to remove impurities.
- Fill the pot with fresh water, add bones, brisket, charred onion, ginger, star anise, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom, fish sauce, sugar, and salt.
- Simmer gently for 2-3 hours, skimming foam as needed.
- Remove brisket when tender, slice thinly, and set aside.
- Strain the broth to remove solids.
- Cook rice noodles according to package instructions.
- To serve: Place noodles in a bowl, add sliced brisket and raw beef (if using), pour hot broth over, and top with fresh herbs, bean sprouts, lime, chili, and sauces as desired.
